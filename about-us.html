We’ll Take You to the Heart of Norway

Up Norway was set up with the modern, independent traveller in mind. If you love breaking new ground, discovering, tasting, exploring and generally enjoying life, then we’re speaking the same language.

Our goal is to help you find your perfect experience in Norway. Our land is rich – not only with oil and fish, but with unique life experiences, and we know exactly how to find the best ones. Over land and sea, we want to expose you to our stunning nature. We’ll put you in touch with our countrymen, serve up our local cuisine, and show off our vibrant culture, from music, art and architecture to winter sports. From Viking ancestry to modern democracy, we want to tell the story of what has shaped today’s successful, happy society based on trust and equality.
Up Norway has access to a store of secret places. We’ll take you off the tourist trail and give you access to sites and stories other tour operators ignore. Our slow and sustainable travel lets you savour the stunning views and magical moments.

Hei, Hello, Welcome!

Just like you, I love to travel. Not to the most popular attractions and selfie-spots, but to where the real, unique experiences are. I prefer things off season and off the beaten track. I seek out genuine people who take pride in what they do and where they come from, and who treat me as their guest rather than just another customer. I like to test my limits, start conversations and try out unfamiliar local food and drink. Up Norway is my way of sharing these experiences with you – in Norway, the coolest place on earth. I grew up in the small town of Kongsvinger, but have always felt the urge to explore. I have studied and worked in Australia, Thailand, the Maldives, United Arab Emirates, Switzerland and Bhutan. I have had the pleasure of working for companies such as Aman Resorts, Jumeirah and Anantara Hotels, and met with many travellers. Many of them are curious about Norway, yet have no idea what the country has to offer other than fjords, salmon and the Northern Lights. Maybe you’re one of them too? Well, that’s about to change. Up Norway is here to show off this diverse land’s finest assets. Travel Up Norway!
Torunn, Founder
