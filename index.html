HOW IT works

UP NORWAY
In 5 Simple Steps

  • Fill in our easy questionnaire – it’s full of ideas and inspirations. Tell us what you like best!
  • Give us a day and our insiders will propose your recommended journey. All trips can be tailored to suit you.
  • Once your trip through Scandi heaven is fine-tuned and approved, we’ll deal with all the logistics!
  • Your trip will appear in a fully interactive digital travel guide. All your essential info in one handy package – and we’ll always be available to chat if you need us!
  • Get yourself up here, and your Norwegian journey can begin!
